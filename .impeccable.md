## Design Context

### Users
Azure Synthetics serves qualified laboratory buyers, research teams, inventory managers, and documentation-first purchasers evaluating research-use peptide materials. Users need to compare products, confirm pack sizes and handling requirements, place orders, and trust that compliance, lot, and CoA workflows are clear before purchase.

### Brand Personality
Premium, clinical, and precise. The interface should feel like a credible biochemistry supplier with polished ecommerce ergonomics, not a wellness brand, supplement storefront, or generic AI-generated lab theme.

### Aesthetic Direction
Use a refined lab-commerce look: warm off-white surfaces, deep blue-green panels, teal and muted gold accents, realistic product/laboratory imagery, restrained typography, and tight operational copy. Mobile must preserve the desktop brand signals, imagery quality, navigation, language support, and trust cues instead of stripping them away.

### Design Principles
- Keep research-use compliance visible without making the storefront feel alarmist.
- Preserve desktop content parity on mobile; adapt layout and touch behavior, do not remove important brand or buying information.
- Favor realistic lab/product visuals and transparent brand assets over fake-looking composites or boxed logo treatments.
- Use consistent tokens, spacing, and component patterns across header, footer, product cards, pages, and WooCommerce surfaces.
- Treat Arabic/RTL as a first-class experience with the same visual quality as English.
